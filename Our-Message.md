ETX: What's Our Message?
Tagline
The science of learning. The art of storytelling. Education for everyone.
Mission Statement
We don't build lessons. We design learning experiences. By combining learning science, storytelling, technology, and human-centered design, we create educational experiences that engage learners, support instructors, and produce meaningful outcomes.
Values
People First
Great learning starts with people. We build genuine relationships with educators and learners, listening carefully to their needs, aspirations, and experiences. We design with people, not just for them.
Curiosity Drives Learning
We believe curiosity, exploration, discovery, and storytelling are at the heart of meaningful learning. We create experiences that invite learners to ask questions, make connections, and uncover understanding for themselves.
Evidence Matters
We ground our work in research and learning science, continually measuring, studying, and improving what we create. We value evidence over assumptions and seek measurable impact for learners and educators.
Education for Everyone
We believe meaningful learning opportunities should be accessible to all. Through open educational practices and thoughtful design, we work to remove barriers and expand access to high-quality educational experiences.
Purposeful Design
We don't build lessons. We design learning experiences. Every element, from technology and narrative to feedback and interaction, should serve a meaningful purpose and support learning.
Human-Centered Innovation
Technology is most powerful when it helps people learn, connect, and grow. We embrace innovation not because it is new or flashy, but because it helps create more engaging, effective, and human educational experiences.
